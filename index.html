MOST VISITED PLACES

TRENDING DESTINATIONS

Gulmarg

Gulmarg, located in Jammu and Kashmir, is renowned for its breathtaking beauty and world-class skiing opportunities, making it a popular destination for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts alike. Surrounded by snow-capped peaks and lush green meadows, Gulmarg offers a serene escape amidst the Himalayas.

Know More

Ladakh

Ladakh, nestled in the northernmost region of India, is known for its stark yet captivating landscapes, including high-altitude deserts, turquoise lakes, and ancient monasteries. It's a haven for adventure seekers and cultural enthusiasts, offering a unique blend of natural beauty and rich heritage.

Know More

manali

Manali, situated in the state of Himachal Pradesh, is a picturesque hill station known for its snow-capped mountains, lush valleys, and gushing rivers, making it a favorite destination for honeymooners and adventure lovers. Its charming villages, apple orchards, and opportunities for trekking and skiing attract tourists throughout the year.

Know More

Shimla

Shimla, the capital city of Himachal Pradesh, is renowned for its colonial architecture, including the iconic Christ Church and Viceregal Lodge, set against the backdrop of the Himalayan foothills. Its pleasant climate, scenic views, and historical charm make it a popular destination for tourists seeking a blend of culture and natural beauty.

Know More

Rishikesh

Rishikesh, nestled in the foothills of the Himalayas along the Ganges River, is famous as the Yoga Capital of the World, offering serene surroundings for spiritual seekers and yoga enthusiasts. Known for its ashrams, adventure sports like river rafting, and the iconic Laxman Jhula suspension bridge, Rishikesh is a vibrant hub of spirituality and adventure.

Know More

udaipur

Udaipur, also known as the "City of Lakes," captivates visitors with its royal heritage, stunning palaces like the Lake Palace and City Palace, and picturesque lakeside settings. Renowned for its rich culture, colorful festivals, and traditional Rajasthani architecture, Udaipur offers a mesmerizing blend of history and natural beauty.

Know More

Amritsar

Amritsar, located in the northwestern Indian state of Punjab, is renowned for its vibrant culture and rich history, including the iconic Golden Temple, a spiritual and architectural marvel attracting millions of visitors each year.

Know More

Jaipur

Jaipur, the capital city of Rajasthan in India, is known for its majestic palaces, vibrant markets, and rich heritage, including the famous Hawa Mahal and the majestic Amer Fort, making it a popular destination for tourists seeking a glimpse of India's royal past.

Know More

Agra

Agra, situated in the northern Indian state of Uttar Pradesh, is renowned for being home to the iconic Taj Mahal, a UNESCO World Heritage site and one of the Seven Wonders of the World. The city's historical significance, coupled with its Mughal architecture and vibrant culture, attracts millions of tourists from around the globe.
